About Us

Mycological Solutions Ltd is built on a simple principle: fungi hold some of the most powerful solutions for the environmental challenges we face today.

We focus on applied environmental mycology and fungal analysis across agricultural, aquatic, and environmental systems. By studying fungal communities and their interactions with natural and human-influenced environments, we help uncover how fungi can be used to better understand, restore, and improve ecological systems.

Our work supports projects that require reliable fungal testing, environmental analysis, and scientific interpretation, connecting laboratory research with practical environmental applications.

Our Approach

Fungi are fundamental drivers of ecological processes. They shape soil health, influence water systems, recycle nutrients, and interact with plants, microbes, and ecosystems in complex ways.

At Mycological Solutions Ltd, we approach mycology as a practical tool for solving real environmental problems. Through careful analysis, clear methodology, and evidence-based interpretation, we aim to translate fungal science into knowledge that can support environmental restoration, sustainable practices, and innovative biological solutions.

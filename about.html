About Us

Mycological Solutions Ltd was established in 2025 with a focus on applied environmental mycology and fungal analysis across agricultural, aquatic, and environmental systems.

The company was founded to translate scientific understanding of fungal ecology into practical tools for environmental monitoring, contamination investigation, and ecosystem analysis. Fungi play a critical role in nutrient cycling, soil health, and aquatic ecosystems, yet their ecological functions remain underutilized in environmental assessment and management.

Mycological Solutions works with projects that require reliable fungal testing, biological interpretation, and environmental analysis. By combining laboratory methods, environmental sampling, and scientific interpretation, the company aims to generate meaningful biological insight that supports research, environmental monitoring, and sustainable ecosystem management.

Although newly established, the company builds on a strong background in environmental microbiology and fungal ecology research.

Applied fungal science for:

  • Agricultural systems
  • Aquatic ecosystems
  • Contamination investigation
